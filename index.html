About Me

As a passionate artist and graphic designer with a Multimedia Graphic Design degree, I’m dedicated to transforming the world through vibrant, innovative art. Originally from Colorado, I’ve recently embraced New Mexico’s creative spirit to fuel my print-on-demand business, showcasing unique designs on Etsy and at local Albuquerque markets. Driven by a vision to explore the world, I craft art inspired by diverse experiences, aiming to captivate and inspire. Discover my work and join my journey to redefine creativity.
